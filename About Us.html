ABOUT US

Our project is inspired by

the struggles of fresh graduates picking up part time jobs due to the difficulty of landing a new job in these recent years. So, we took to our hand to develop a platform where employees and employers alike can advertise themselves as a competent workforce or a prospective workplace for young adults to learn new things and hone their skills. The traditional way of job seeking is very time-consuming and tiring, therefore we hope that our website can be akin to a ray of light at the end of the tunnel for those who feel hopeless.
