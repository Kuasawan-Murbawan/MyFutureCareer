Job Description

Account Executive



ADS is the trading name of ADS International Express Sdn Bhd. ADS is an established Malaysian based logistics company & licensed courier provider and we work closely with world leading airlines, partners and logistics companies both from the Far East and European countries. We specialize in airfreight forwarding, international door to door courier delivery, trucking, warehouse facilities, distribution, order fulfillments, cargo insurance and strategic consultation. We value strong relationship building with our partners and clients, and all members of the company take pride in what we do and are always prepared and committed in adopting hands-on approach in our day to day work providing personalized service to our customers.
