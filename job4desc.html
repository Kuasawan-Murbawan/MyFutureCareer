Job Description

Forwarding Import Executive


Fully understand procedure on handling and manage Import Shipment Flow such as open job by receiving document (Bill of Landing/ Invoice / Packing List, Etc), Prepare and sending ROT (Request of Transport) to Transportation Company. Have experience on communicate with liner on requesting NOA (Notice of Arrival / Invoice), in advance persuade liner early release Delivery Order / EDO before shipment arrival.

  • 2+ years of Forwarding Agent Customer Service experience is essential for this role.
  • Experience dealing with both Imports and Exports is beneficial.
  • Knowledge of customs regulations would be desirable
  • The ideal candidate will be commercially minded.
  • Exceptional communication skills are required for this role.
  • Candidates fluent in Mandarin preferred as role requires candidate to deal with Mandarin speaking clients.
