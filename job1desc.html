Job Description

Operations (Generalist) Executive


What you will be doing:

  • Engaging with Kaodim’s service providers to ensure they are aware of all functions to deliver the best experience possible and to take part in driving optimum value from the Company or variations thereof
  • Communicating with Kaodim vendor network to build good working relationship and provide support via in-house standard operating procedures
  • Oversee and handling any incoming appointments, schedules for Kaodim’s service providers and making changes when necessary
  • Handling incoming calls and enquiries from customers with a demonstration of excellent customer service skills
  • Collaborating with the Company’s Operations Team and other key personnel in implementing improvements in processes and policies to ensure that the Company continues to deliver value and quality results
